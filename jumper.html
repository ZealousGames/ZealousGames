Green Square

Green Square is a game by ZealousGames where you use the arrow keys to control a green square. Your goal is to score points by moving over the red squares, before the timer of 20 seconds runs out. Each red square adds one point to your score. The game also has cool features like coordinates. The timer will start once you make your first movement. Can you beat 8?
